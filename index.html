Since 2012

Kauai's Most Trusted Plumbers

Blue Pipe Plumbing was founded by master plumber Keali'i Akana, a Kauai native who spent 10 years working for large plumbing contractors before starting his own company with a simple philosophy: do the work right, treat customers like family, and always be honest about what needs to be done.

Today we're a team of 8 licensed plumbers serving all of Kauai, with special expertise in the unique challenges of island plumbing — hard water, corrosive salt air, and the high demands of Hawaii's tourism-driven vacation rental market.

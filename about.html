About Blue Pipe Plumbing

Kauai-born, community-driven, excellence-committed.

Est. 2012

Born from Kauai, Built for Kauai

Keali'i Akana grew up in Lihue watching his father fix things — water heaters, leaky pipes, the stuff that makes a home work. After earning his Master Plumber license and spending a decade in larger operations, Keali'i saw an opportunity to build something Kauai was missing: a plumbing company run with genuine aloha — honest pricing, clear communication, and a team that actually cares.

Blue Pipe Plumbing launched in 2012 with one truck and one employee. Today we operate a fleet of 6 service vehicles and a team of 8 licensed plumbers. We've earned our reputation one job at a time, and we protect it with every call we answer.
